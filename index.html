Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you travel

How do I book a tour package? ▼

Booking a tour is simple! Browse our packages on the website, select the one you like, and click "Book Now". Fill in your travel dates and personal details, then confirm your booking. Our team will send you a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Can I customise my tour package? ▼

Absolutely! We offer fully customisable tour packages. You can choose your destination, number of days, hotel category, meals, transport, and sightseeing options. Use our Tour Package Builder on the website to design your perfect trip and get an instant price estimate.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

We offer flexible cancellation options. If you cancel more than 30 days before departure, you receive a full refund. Cancellations between 15–30 days receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer a refund for cancellations within 15 days of the travel date. Please contact our support team for special circumstances.

Is travel insurance included in the package? ▼

Travel insurance is not included by default, but we strongly recommend it. We have partnered with trusted insurance providers and you can easily add travel insurance during the booking process. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage.

How do I contact customer support? ▼

Our support team is available 7 days a week from 9 AM to 8 PM. You can reach us via email at support@travelja.com, call us on +91 98765 43210, or use the live chat on our website. We aim to respond to all queries within a few hours.

Still have questions? Contact our team — we're happy to help!
